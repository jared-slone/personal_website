Selected Research

Language Models Geometric DL

STAG-LLM

A multimodal model combining protein language models with geometric deep learning. We predict TCR–pHLA binding by leveraging both sequence data and computationally generated 3D structures, outperforming existing methods even with limited data.

View Publication
Structural Biology Sampling

STEGG

Structural TCR-pMHC Ensemble Generator and Gallery. A computational framework that generates distinct low-energy 3D conformations to capture the flexible nature of immune interactions, going beyond static structure generation.

View Publication
Computer Vision Clinical Deployment

Covid-19 Diagnostic AI

Developed a ResNet-50 based computer vision solution for detecting COVID-19 in chest CT scans to aid radiologists. Deployed via cloud infrastructure and utilized in over 15 states during the pandemic peak with 0.99 sensitivity.
